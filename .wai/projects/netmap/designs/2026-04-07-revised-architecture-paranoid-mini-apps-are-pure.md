Revised architecture: paranoid mini-apps are pure native Android — Kotlin + Android Views (XML layouts), no Jetpack Compose, no Hilt, no heavy DI. Minimal dependencies per app. Each mini-app is an Activity or Fragment launched from the hub. Manual dependency wiring (constructor injection or simple ServiceLocator). The goal is low memory, small APK, fast startup. The WebView hub is dropped — the main screen is a native RecyclerView listing available apps.
